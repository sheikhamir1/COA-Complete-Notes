1.5 Conversion Tricks

Converting between number systems is easier than you might think! Here's how it works:

üî¢‚û°Ô∏èüíªDecimal to Binary

13 √∑ 2 = 6 remainder 1
6 √∑ 2 = 3 remainder 0
3 √∑ 2 = 1 remainder 1
1 √∑ 2 = 0 remainder 1

Reading the remainders backward: 13 ‚Üí 1101

üíª‚û°Ô∏èüî£Binary to Octal

üíª‚û°Ô∏èüî°Binary to Hexadecimal
